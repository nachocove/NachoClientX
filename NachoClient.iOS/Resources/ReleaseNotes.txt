Nacho Mail 2.0 adds support for multiple email accounts, IMAP, and Google 2-factor authentication. Now you can add all of your Gmail, Yahoo, iCloud email accounts, or any email account that supports IMAP like AOL, to Nacho Mail.  It's easy to add or switch accounts -- just press the button in the middle of the top of the screen.  Of course, Nacho Mail continues to support Exchange, Hotmail, Office 365, and Google Apps for Work.

Calendar and Contacts now sync with your phone to give you a single unified view of people and events from Nacho Mail.
