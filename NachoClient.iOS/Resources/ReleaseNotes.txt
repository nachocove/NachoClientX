This pre-beta release addresses the following issues:
1) On Exchange 2007 and Hotmail, messages in list view contained no preview lines of message.
2) Certain username combinations for Exchange users were not recognized.
3) Visually distinguish between read/unread messages.
4) UI stalled on app startup for Google and Hotmail users.
5) Certain crashes related to slow Exchange server responses.
6) Ability to reach and modify server settings in advanced settings.
7) Faster load times of messages.
8) Certain calendar items were displayed poorly.
9) Other internal fixes and optimizations.

Known issues that are not addressed in this release
1) Slow app launch times.
2) Duplicate entries for contacts.
3) Drafts folder for messages that are not completed.
